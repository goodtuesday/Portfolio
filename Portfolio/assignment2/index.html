Who is Eric Sievers?

He earned a Juris Doctor from Elon University in 2015 and completed his bachelors degree in 2009 at Appalachian State University as a philosophy and psychology double major. He is currently learning web development through The Coding Bootcamp at UNC Chapel Hill.
